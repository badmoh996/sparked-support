## Common Questions

### How do I cancel my Sparked Pro subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID settings:

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Tap Sparked
5. Tap Cancel Subscription

### How do I delete my account?

Open Sparked, go to the Me tab, tap Your profile, then Delete account. This will permanently remove your data from our servers.

### How do I change notification times?

Open Sparked, go to the Me tab, tap Your profile, then tap Morning notification or Evening reminder to set your preferred time.

### I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, contact support at badmoh996@gmail.com and we will help you reset it.

### My streak isn't updating

Make sure you complete the evening check-in before midnight in your local time zone. If the issue persists, please email us with details.
